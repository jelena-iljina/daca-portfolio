# Andmelugu

This dashboard analyzes UrbanStyle’s e-commerce performance in 2023–2024.  
It shows that the online channel generated €925.18K in revenue from 3K orders with an average order value of €289.94.  
Online sales grew by 37.85% in 2024 compared to 2023, showing strong development of the digital sales channel.  
The dashboard also highlights monthly sales trends and the top products driving online revenue.
