# 📊 Week 6 – Visualization Data

## 👩‍💻 My Role

My role was **Role D: E-commerce Story**. My task was to create an interactive dashboard and a short business narrative focused on the **online sales channel**. The goal of this role was to show the **growth dynamics of e-commerce**, highlight its business potential, and explain why this channel is important for UrbanStyle’s future.

## ✅ My Tasks

As part of this role, I worked with the **online sales data** and focused only on the `channel = online` view. My main tasks were:

- create an e-commerce dashboard with key KPIs  
- analyze monthly sales trends  
- identify top-performing products  
- show the share of e-commerce in total revenue  
- add storytelling elements such as annotations and a reference line  
- write a short business narrative with conclusions and recommendations  

## 🤝 What We Did as a Team

As a team, we analyzed UrbanStyle’s business from several perspectives: Tallinn store, Tartu store, Pärnu store, and e-commerce. Each team member prepared one location or channel view, and then we combined the results into one shared business story. This helped us compare performance across locations, identify common patterns, and prepare a combined executive view.

## 💡 Main Business Conclusions

Our team analysis showed that the company’s performance is strongly influenced by **seasonality**, location-specific patterns, and channel differences. Physical stores remain important, but their results vary by region and customer behavior. At the same time, **e-commerce stands out as a fast-growing and strategically important channel**, already contributing a significant share of total revenue.

The combined view suggests that UrbanStyle should continue investing in **high-performing channels**, especially online sales, while also improving customer retention and reducing seasonal risk in weaker periods. The analysis also showed that a relatively small number of top products generate a large share of sales, which makes product focus and inventory planning especially important.
