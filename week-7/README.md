## Role A: Data Loading and Integration

My role in this project was data loading and integration.

First, I connected to the Supabase database using Python and loaded the `sales` and `customers` tables into pandas DataFrames. After that, I merged the datasets into one combined table containing both sales and customer information.

The final result was a unified dataset that could be used by the other team members for further data cleaning, transformation, and analysis.

### Key Insight
One of the main findings was that the company has a strong base of repeat customers, since more than 10,000 sales records were linked to only around 3,000 customers.

### Reflection
What surprised us the most was how many repeat purchases were made by the same customers and how large the sales dataset was compared to the size of the customer base.

This suggests strong customer loyalty and active buying behavior.
