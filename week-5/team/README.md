## Team Conclusion

Based on our presentation, the main conclusion was that **Urban Style showed a clear sales growth trend during 2023–2024**. Total revenue reached **€2.91M**, the customer base grew to **3.15K**, and annual sales growth was **19%**.

## Key Results

Our team found that sales have strong **seasonal peaks**, especially at the end of the year, where revenue reached around **€170K** compared to **€85K–95K** in weaker months. This shows high monthly volatility and highlights the business need for better planning of **inventory, workforce, and marketing campaigns** during peak and low seasons.
